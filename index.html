Who Am I?

I am a Data Analyst with over four years of experience transforming raw data into actionable business insights. My work focuses on collecting, cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing data to support strategic decision-making across business operations. I have experience working with structured and unstructured datasets, developing interactive dashboards, automating reporting processes, and uncovering trends that drive measurable outcomes. Alongside my professional experience, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Data Science, where I continue to strengthen my knowledge of statistics, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and advanced analytical techniques. This combination of practical industry experience and academic training enables me to approach complex problems with both technical precision and analytical thinking. I believe that data is more than numbers. Every dataset tells a story, and my goal is to translate that story into insights that help organizations improve efficiency, optimize performance, reduce costs, and make informed decisions. I am passionate about continuous learning, emerging AI technologies, and building data-driven solutions that create real business value.
